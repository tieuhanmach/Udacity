Relationship between Delay Time and other Flight's characteristics¶

by Ha Nguyen¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the relationship between flights delay time and characteristics of flights to see if they have any interesting partern. The main focus was on the five reason of Delay: CarrierDelay, WeatherDelay, NASDelay, SecurityDelay, LateAircraftDelay.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consitst of 7009724 unique records about flights in the United States in 2008, including date of flight, actual and scheduled arrival time, carriers, airport code, arrival and departure delays time, and reasons for delays. 4 duplicated records have been removed and one new atribute - Date in the full format yyyy-MM-DD has been created. The five reasons in seperated columns have been transformed to 2 new atributes: Reason and Time

Distribution of Arrival Delay Time¶

Arrival Delay Time in the dataset take on a very large range of values, from about -500 mins at the lowest, to about 2500 mins at the highest. Plotted on a logarithmic scale, data still has right skewed distributions, with 2 peaks on 10 and 20 min.
